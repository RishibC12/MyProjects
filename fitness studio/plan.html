Membership plans


Standard Membership: This membership plan includes unlimited access to the gym during normal facility hours. It also includes access to the cardio and weight machines, as well as access to group classes.

Premium Membership: This membership plan includes all the benefits of the Standard Membership, plus access to the sauna, steam room, and hot tub, as well as free personal training sessions.

Deluxe Membership: This membership plan includes all the benefits of the Premium Membership, plus access to exclusive classes, discounts on additional services and products, and a monthly massage.
